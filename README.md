# Bright.tv-Project-19-April-2026

This project focuses on analysing viewer behaviour and demographics to provide the Customer Value Management (CVM) team at Bright TV with actionable insights. The primary objective was to identify strategies to grow the subscription base for the current financial year by understanding what drives viewership.
--------------------
Technical Workflow
---------------------
1. Data Cleaning & Integration
The raw datasets required significant preprocessing before analysis.
Cleaning: Initial data cleaning was performed on individual sheets to handle formatting inconsistencies and obvious errors.
Merging: The cleaned sheets were uploaded to Databricks, where a Left Join was executed to consolidate the disparate data into a single, unified master table.
2. Analysis & Visualisation
The unified dataset was ingested into Excel to leverage Pivot Tables for deep-dive analysis. Key areas of focus included:
Consumption Patterns: Identifying peak viewing times and duration.
Content Trends: Mapping the most popular TV channels and genres.
Demographics: Segmenting the audience by gender and race.
------------------------
Key Findings & Insights
------------------------
Gender Dominance: The data reveals that males are the primary audience drivers for current subscriptions.
Content Powerhouse: Sports channels are the leading force behind viewership and retention, consistently outperforming other genres.
Demographic Leads: Specific racial and gender segments were identified as the core user base, providing a clear target for marketing campaigns.
Strategic Recommendations
Leverage Sports Content: Since sports are the main driver, CVM should focus on sports-centric acquisition offers and retention "save" tactics.
Targeted Marketing: Align promotional spend with the identified dominant demographics (Male/Sports-focused) while exploring "lookalike" audiences to expand the base.
Data Quality Initiative: A critical takeaway is the need for improved Data Collection. A significant portion of the dataset was unusable due to "null" or "unknown" demographics. To improve future precision, Bright TV must incentivise customers to complete their profiles.
